//TOPIC: Box Model (Margin, Padding, Height, Width, Box Model //! Margin Collapse) --

Margin is the space outside the border of an element.

It creates space between elements.

//HEADING: Margin sides: --
margin-top
margin-right
margin-bottom
margin-left

//HEADING: Shorthand order: --

Top → Right → Bottom → Left

//HEADING: Examples: --

//LITENOTE: margin:20px;  --
(all sides)

//LITENOTE: margin:20px 10px; --
(top-bottom | left-right)

//LITENOTE: margin:20px 10px 5px; --
(top | left-right | bottom)

//LITENOTE: margin:20px 10px 5px 15px; --
(top | right | bottom | left)

//IMPORTANT: margin:auto is used to center block elements horizontally. --
