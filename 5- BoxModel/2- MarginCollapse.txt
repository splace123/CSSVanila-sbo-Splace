//TOPIC: Margin Collapse in CSS --




Margin collapse happens when vertical margins combine.

Only top and bottom margins collapse.

Left and right margins never collapse.

Collapsed margin = larger margin value.

//HEADING: Example: --
20px + 20px = 20px (not 40px)

//HEADING: To prevent margin collapse: --
use padding
use border
use overflow

//IMPORTANT: Important Rule --

Margin collapse sirf vertical margins mein hota hai.

✔ Top margin
✔ Bottom margin

❌ Left margin
❌ Right margin

Left aur right kabhi collapse nahi hote.
