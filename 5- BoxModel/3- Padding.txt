Padding is the space inside the border of an element.

It creates space between content and border.

//HEADING: Padding sides: --
padding-top
padding-right
padding-bottom
padding-left

//HEADING: Shorthand order: --
Top → Right → Bottom → Left

//HEADING: Examples: --

padding:20px;
(all sides)

padding:20px 10px;
(top-bottom | left-right)

padding:20px 10px 5px;
(top | left-right | bottom)

padding:20px 10px 5px 15px;
(top | right | bottom | left)
